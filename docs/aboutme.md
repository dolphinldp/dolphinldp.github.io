# Dolphin Liu 
 **Systems Architect | Hardware STEM teacher | Certified Scrum Master**


## Professional Profile 👋

Currently an active student in the MIT Fab Academy 2026, specializing in advanced digital fabrication and rapid prototyping.

Systems engineer and technical lead with a focus on web systems , embedded systems (ESP32/STM32) and full-stack architecture. 

Experienced in hardware STEM project ,and STEM course for school students and teachers.

Guiding teams from initial concepts to functional hardware MVPs. I bridge the gap between complex software logic and physical hardware through agile methodologies and hands-on technical coaching.

## Core Competencies 🛠️

- Software Architecture: Strong background in Java (Enterprise Systems) and Web technologies (React, Node.js, Three.js) for hardware-software integration.With a bundle of experience in web system development.

- Embedded Systems & IoT: Proficiency in ESP32,  Arduino, and RP2040. With a bundle of experience in STEM hardware development.

- Digital Fabrication: Hands-on workflow expertise in 3D design, CNC machining, laser cutting, and precision PCB milling (Fab Academy ).

- Project Leadership: Certified Scrum Master (CSM). Skilled in applying Agile frameworks to hardware development to manage timelines and technical risks in startup environments.
